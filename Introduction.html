Currently, Smartphone is an extremely popular device with humans, we use them to serve our life, entertainment purposes, work, take pictures, record videos,...

Concept

    A smartphone is a type of mobile phone with an integrated operating system that makes a phone much more versatile. Mobile phones have always developed in two trends. 1 is very small, convenient to carry everywhere. 2 is to combine the tablet between the phone and the computer.
    Cell phones are usually pocket-sized, often incorporating the features of a regular phone such as making and receiving regular calls, receiving text prompts, and other text-communicating features such as a mobile phone. music player, calendar, calculator, games, camera and even video recording, Most mobile phones these days can access the internet and install many 3rd party applications in Play Store or App Store.

History

    Mobile phones were officially launched and accepted in 1999 and have been widely used since 2000. Most mobile phones manufactured from 2012 onwards can use 3G and 4G. As of the third quarter of 2012, 1 billion phones have been sold worldwide. As of this year, 65% of mobile phone users are smartphones. By 2016, this number accounted for 79% of the mobile market. And now, most mobile phones run iOS and Android operating systems.
